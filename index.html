FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Quick, direct answers about service area, complete remodels, licensing and how to get started.

Does TJN Services LLC do complete home remodels?

Yes. TJN Services LLC handles complete home remodels and multi-room projects from start to finish — framing, drywall, flooring, tile, painting, kitchens and bathrooms — managed under one contractor instead of multiple subcontractors.

What areas does TJN Services LLC serve?

TJN Services LLC serves homeowners across King County and Snohomish County, Washington, including Edmonds, Lynnwood, Mountlake Terrace, Mill Creek, Everett, Shoreline, Bothell and Seattle.

Is TJN Services LLC licensed?

Yes. TJN Services LLC is a licensed contractor in Washington State (License #TJNSESL774R2) with over 10 years of experience in remodeling, drywall, flooring, tile and painting.

What flooring options does TJN Services LLC install?

TJN Services LLC installs LVP (luxury vinyl plank), laminate and hardwood flooring, along with tile flooring for kitchens, bathrooms and entryways.

How do I get a free estimate?

Contact TJN Services LLC by phone, email or the contact form with your project details and location. Most homeowners receive a response within a few hours and a clear estimate after a quick review of the scope of work.
